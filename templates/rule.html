How to Play
Guess the hidden Wordle Word in six tries.

Each guess must be a valid five-letter word. Hit enter or submit button to validate the guessed word.

After each guess, the color of the tiles will change to show how close your guess was to the word.

Example:

            

Letter "W" is in the word in the correct position.

Letter "S" is present , but in different place.

Letters "W/O/D" are not in the word in any spot.
